<p>Texas Hold Em is the world's most popular version of poker.</p>

<p>Each player receives some number of chips, representing their money, that they bet with.</p>
<h2>Chip Values</h2>
<ul>
<li>White: $1</li>
<li>Red: $5</li>
<li>Blue: $10</li>
<li>Green: $25</li>
<li>Black: $100</li>
<li>Purple: $500</li>
</ul>
<p>Source: <a href="https://www.poker.org/poker-chip-values/">poker.org/poker-chip-values</a></p>

<h2>Poker Hands</h2>
<ol>
  <li>
    Royal Flush
  A, K, Q, J, 10, all in the same suit.
    </li>
  <li>
    Straight Flush
    5 cards in a sequence, all in the same suit.
  </li>
  <li>
    Four of a Kind
    4 cards with the same rank (value).
  </li>
  <li>
    Full House
    Three of a kind with a pair.
  </li>
</ol>

<h3>Game Flow</h3>
The game starts with chips being distributed to each person. Then a hand starts.

A hand starts with a "dealer" being assigned (though, in CardHub, we distribute the cards for you).
